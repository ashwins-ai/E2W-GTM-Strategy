## Notes on Usage

- Government and policy documents were used to validate infrastructure readiness, incentives, and regulatory direction.  
- Industry reports were referenced for adoption trends, city-level EV growth, and charging ecosystem maturity.  
- Journalism sources were used to triangulate market behaviour and Tier-2/Tier-3 demand patterns.  
- Educational and urbanisation datasets informed institutional density and youth-driven mobility demand.  

All interpretations and strategic conclusions in this repository are derived through synthesis of these sources rather than direct reproduction of proprietary content.
